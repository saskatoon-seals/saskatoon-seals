Saskatoon Seals

We are underwater hockey players, we come in peace.

We are a crazy team of underwater football and hockey players. They say we are a recreational club, however we have two experienced coaches training us. We train hard in the pool and play fun after that - all of us are friends who hang out a lot also when not in the pool. We are from six different continents of the world and everyone brings a special spice to our great team. Welcome joining us!
